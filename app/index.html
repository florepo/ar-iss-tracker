AR ISS Tracker

An Augmented Reality model of the International Space Station's current position

How it works ...

View the current positon of the International Space Station through a 3D augmented reality model.

The positon is calculated based on up-to-date positional data in real time.

The model can be viewed by scanning the trigger image with the AR viewer.

How to use it ...

ONEOpen the AR Viewer

  • Scan the QR code with your mobile phone camera - either from a print-out, or directly from this website - to open the AR viewer.

    When opening the AR viewer you will need to grant the page permission to access your camera.

  • Access the QR code for opening the AR viewer here.
  • Alternatively, download the QR code for printing here.

TWOScan the Trigger Image

  • Once the AR viewer is open scan the trigger image with your mobile phone camera.
  • Open the trigger image for scanning here.
  • Alternatively, download the image for printing here.

THREEView the AR model

  • An 3D model of the earth with appear above the trigger images, showing the live position of the International Space Station.

    The augmented reality model is visible as long the trigger image is in the view of the mobile's camera.
