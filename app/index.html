View an Augmented Reality model of the current positon of the International Space Station through.

The model can be viewed with the AR Viewer by scanning the trigger image .

How to use it ...

  • Open the AR viewer and grant the application permission to access the camera of your device

  • Launch the AR viewer directly from this website
  • Open the AR viewer by scanning this QR code on your mobile device

  • Once the AR viewer is open scan the trigger image.

  • Open the trigger image for scanning directly on from this site
  • Download the image for printing

  • An 3D model of the earth with appear above the trigger images, showing the live position of the International Space Station.

    The positon is calculated based on up-to-date orbital positional data.

    The model is visible as long the trigger image is in the view of the mobile phone's camera.
