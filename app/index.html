AR ISS Tracker

An Augmented Reality model of the International Space Station's current position

Shows the current position of International Space Station (ISS) as an Augmented Reality model.

The ISS's position is calculated based on up-to-date orbital data (Three Line Elements) from Celestrack.

Technologies used: jsartoolkit5, three.js, satellite.js, Bootstrap, Github Workflow CI, Terraform, and AWS.

How to view the model

  • Scan the trigger image 2x with the camera of your mobile device:

  • First, scan the QR code to open the web app's AR viewer.
  • Second, scan the whole image - with the camera view of the web app's AR viewer - to display the model.
